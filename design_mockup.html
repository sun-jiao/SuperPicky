‚ú¶ Design Philosophy

Minimalism

Remove all visual noise - no emojis, no gradients, no shadows except where functionally necessary. Let the content breathe with generous whitespace.

Typography

Inter for UI labels (humanist, highly legible), JetBrains Mono for data and logs. Strict typographic hierarchy through weight and size.

Color

Monochromatic base with a single accent color (cyan-green). Color is used sparingly and meaningfully - only for interaction states and important data.

Hierarchy

Clear visual hierarchy through subtle background variations. Cards and sections defined by color shift, not borders or shadows.

Motion

Subtle, purposeful animations. The pulsing status indicator and smooth hover states add life without distraction.

Data Display

Monospace numbers for easy scanning. Star ratings use understated gold. Statistics presented in a clean grid layout.
